Hungarian History

Hungary began with the Magyars settling in the Carpathian Basin in the 9th century. Under Saint Stephen, who was crowned in 1000 AD, Hungary became a Christian kingdom.

It faced invasions from the Ottoman Empire and later became part of the Austro-Hungarian Empire in the 19th century. After World War I, the empire dissolved, leaving Hungary to face significant challenges and changes.

Its history is marked by resilience and adaptation throughout various shifts in power and influence.
